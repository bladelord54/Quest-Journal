### Full Description

**Your life is an adventure. Start playing it like one.**

Life Quest Journal transforms your daily tasks, habits, and long-term goals into an epic RPG experience. Every task you complete earns XP, gold, and loot. Every habit you build strengthens your streak. Every goal you crush is a boss defeated.

**QUEST SYSTEM**
Organize your life across 6 tiers — from daily tasks to life-changing goals. Watch your progress cascade upward as small wins fuel massive achievements.

**BOSS BATTLES**
Your weekly and monthly goals become bosses to fight. Complete tasks to earn attack charges, then strike down bosses for epic loot drops — spells, companions, themes, and gold.

**HABIT TRACKING WITH TEETH**
Build daily habits with visible streaks. Miss a day? Your streak breaks — unless your companion or enchantment protects it. Real stakes, real motivation.

**LEVEL UP & EARN REWARDS**
Gain XP from everything you do. Level up to unlock new features, titles, badges, and treasure chests. Open chests for random loot including rare companions and powerful spells.

**SPELLBOOK & ENCHANTMENTS**
Cast spells for XP boosts, gold multipliers, and streak shields. Purchase enchantments with Focus Crystals earned from timed focus sessions.

**COMPANIONS**
Collect and level up companions that give you passive bonuses — extra XP, bonus gold, streak protection, and more.

**FOCUS TIMER**
Built-in Pomodoro-style timer that rewards you with Focus Crystals for staying locked in.

**QUEST CHAINS**
Multi-chapter adventures with progressive rewards. Complete all chapters for legendary loot.

**BEAUTIFUL THEMES**
Unlock color themes as you progress. Customize your journal to match your style.

**100% FREE CORE EXPERIENCE**
All core features are free forever. Optional premium unlocks boss battles, enchantments, and advanced analytics.

**WORKS OFFLINE**
Full PWA support. Your data stays on your device. No account required.

Download Life Quest Journal and start turning your goals into victories.

- My daily habits have streaks — and they break if I skip a day (unless my companion protects me)

Happy to answer questions or take feedback. I built this because I needed it, and I figured others might too.

### r/gamification

**Title:** Built a full RPG layer on top of a productivity app — boss battles, loot drops, companions, spells

Hey everyone. I've been working on Life Quest Journal — a productivity app that adds deep gamification mechanics on top of goal tracking:

**The systems:**
- **XP & Leveling** — Everything earns XP. Level up to unlock features, titles, and treasure chests.
- **Boss Battles** — Weekly/monthly goals become bosses. Complete tasks → earn attack charges → fight the boss → get loot.
- **Loot System** — Weighted rarity (common → legendary). Drops include spells, companions, themes, and gold.
- **Companions** — Collectible creatures with passive bonuses (XP boost, gold boost, streak protection). They level up too.
- **Spellbook** — Consumable spells for 2x XP, gold multipliers, streak shields, etc.
- **Enchantments** — Purchasable buffs using Focus Crystals earned from a built-in Pomodoro timer.
- **Habit Streaks** — Daily habits with streak counters. Miss a day = streak breaks (unless protected).
- **Quest Chains** — Multi-chapter quests with escalating rewards.

I tried to make every mechanic actually serve the productivity goal rather than just being cosmetic. The boss battle system in particular has been great for keeping me focused on weekly targets.

Free on Android — would love to hear thoughts from this community on the game design side.
