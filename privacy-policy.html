Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 4, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Quest Journal ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices. This Privacy Policy explains how Quest Journal handles your information when you use our RPG-themed task management application.

TL;DR: We don't collect, transmit, or store any of your data on our servers. Everything stays on your device.

What Data We Collect

Quest Journal operates entirely on your device using browser storage technology (localStorage). We collect and store the following data locally on your device only:

  • Tasks, goals, and habits you create
  • Your experience points (XP), gold, and level
  • Focus timer sessions and crystals earned
  • Spells, enchantments, and achievements unlocked
  • Boss battles and quest chains you create
  • Calendar events and streaks
  • App settings and preferences (theme, timezone, etc.)
  • Tutorial completion status

Important: All this data is stored exclusively on your device. We never transmit this data to our servers or any third parties.

How We Use Your Data

Since all data remains on your device, we use it solely to:

  • Display your tasks, goals, and progress
  • Calculate your XP, levels, and achievements
  • Track your habit streaks and completion rates
  • Save your preferences and settings
  • Enable offline functionality through our service worker
  • Provide you with a personalized task management experience

Data Storage & Security

Local Storage Only

All your data is stored in your browser's localStorage. This means your data never leaves your device unless you explicitly export it.

No Cloud Backup

We do not provide cloud backup services. If you uninstall the app or clear your browser data, your progress will be lost unless you've exported your data.

Export Feature

You can export your data as a JSON file at any time from the Tools & Settings page. We recommend doing this regularly as a backup.

Third-Party Services

Quest Journal uses the following third-party services that may collect anonymous usage data:

  • Tailwind CSS CDN: For styling (hosted by cdn.tailwindcss.com)
  • Remix Icon CDN: For icons (hosted by cdn.jsdelivr.net)
  • Google Fonts: For custom fonts (hosted by fonts.googleapis.com)

These services may collect basic analytics like page loads, but they do not have access to your Quest Journal data. Please refer to their respective privacy policies for more information.

Analytics & Tracking

We do NOT use any analytics, tracking, or monitoring tools.

We do not track your usage, collect metrics, or analyze your behavior. We have no idea what tasks you create, how often you use the app, or any other usage patterns.

Data Sharing

We do NOT share your data with anyone. Period.

Since your data never leaves your device, there is nothing to share. We do not sell, rent, or trade your information with third parties for marketing or any other purposes.

Your Rights & Control

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: View all your data anytime within the app
  • Export: Download your entire data as a JSON file
  • Delete: Clear all data from Tools & Settings, or uninstall the app
  • Modify: Edit or delete any tasks, goals, or data at any time

Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Quest Journal is safe for users of all ages, including children under 13. Since we don't collect any personal information or transmit data to servers, we comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

Parents can confidently allow their children to use Quest Journal knowing that no personal data is collected or shared.

International Users (GDPR Compliance)

Quest Journal respects the privacy rights of users in the European Union and complies with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

Since we don't collect, process, or store personal data on servers, most GDPR requirements don't apply. However, you still have full rights to access, export, and delete your locally-stored data at any time.

Browser Notifications

Quest Journal may request permission to send browser notifications for:

  • Daily task reminders
  • Enchantment expiration alerts
  • Focus timer completion

You can grant or deny this permission at any time through your browser settings. Notifications are generated locally on your device and do not involve any external servers.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Since the app doesn't check for updates automatically, we recommend reviewing this policy periodically.
